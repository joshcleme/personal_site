Sensing to Decrease Hypoglycemic Events

This project works to alert individuals with Type 1 Diabetes of nocturnal hypoglycemic events to prevent negtaive health effects caused by such events. Our team is utilizing sensors to collect heart rate, body temperature, and moisture levels in order to noninvasively detect an oncoming nocturnal hypoglyemic event.
